Accessibility and Inclusion as Core Mission

Accessibility and inclusion are at the core of our mission. We understand how important it is for everyone, regardless of their physical or cognitive abilities, to have access to the Internet. That is why we design our websites according to the strictest accessibility guidelines, such as WCAG, to ensure that your website is usable by everyone. At Amilio, we believe that technology should be a tool for breaking down barriers and that an accessible website is a powerful tool for inclusion.
