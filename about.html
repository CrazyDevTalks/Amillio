About Us

Your Partners in Digital Innovation

At Amilio, it's about more than just websites—we're here to make dreams come true. What began in Antwerp, Belgium, as an idea from two students with a shared passion for technology and creative design has now grown into a thriving company focused on making high-quality digital solutions accessible. Our journey is one of courage and determination, and we aim to pass those same values on to our clients. Whether you are just starting your entrepreneurial adventure or are an established name looking to enhance your online presence, at Amilio, we believe that every vision deserves to come to life online.

Our Mission

Our mission is to democratize websites and digital access. We want every business, regardless of size or budget, to have the opportunity to present itself professionally and powerfully online. To us, a website is more than just a tool to make a business visible online; it's a chance to tell a story, your story, and to do so in a way that reaches everyone.

That’s why we prioritize accessibility. We design and build websites that meet WCAG standards, making them accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. Accessibility is central to our work because we believe every user, regardless of physical or cognitive ability, should enjoy a smooth and positive digital experience.

Our Vision.

Our vision is of an internet that is fully accessible to everyone. We believe in a future where every website meets the highest standards of accessibility and inclusivity. Websites should be more than just aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly; they must be accessible to all, regardless of physical or cognitive abilities.

That’s why we design with details that enhance accessibility, such as intuitive navigation, clear color contrasts, and screen reader support. Accessible websites not only provide a better user experience but also enable businesses to reach a wider audience, fostering inclusion and diversity in their customer base.

Our Philosophy

Our work is driven by a philosophy that is simple yet powerful: it’s not just about technology, it’s about people. We believe that behind every success story is a vision, and it’s our mission to help shape that vision. We do this through a combination of creative thinking, technical expertise, and a deep understanding of our clients' needs.

Accessibility is not an extra step, but an integral part of our design process. Websites that are accessible to everyone contribute to a fairer and more inclusive internet. By focusing on inclusivity, we ensure that your website not only welcomes everyone but also conveys your brand’s values in a positive and thoughtful way.

Together, we can make your online dreams a reality while building a future where every user, regardless of their abilities, enjoys engaging with your digital platform.
